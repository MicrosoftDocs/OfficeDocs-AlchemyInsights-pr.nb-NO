- [Outlook-2016: Sikkerhetsoppdatering November KB](https://support.microsoft.com/help/4461506)
- [Outlook 2013: Sikkerhetsoppdatering November KB](https://support.microsoft.com/help/4461486)
- [Outlook 2010: Sikkerhetsoppdatering November KB](https://support.microsoft.com/help/4461585) 

Du kan se den nyeste statusen for dette kjente problemet ved å gå [feil i Outlook: "operasjonen mislyktes..." Når du velger Behandle regler & varsler](https://support.office.com/article/Outlook-Error-The-operation-failed-when-selecting-Manage-Rules-Alerts-64b6ff77-98c2-4564-9cbf-25bd8e17fb8b%20).
