# <a name="add-update-or-remove-your-payment-methods"></a>Legge til, oppdatere eller fjerne betalingsmetoder

Du kan kjøpe produkter og tjenester fra Microsoft med **kredittkort, debetkort eller bankkonto**. Du kan angi betalingsinformasjon på siden [Betalingsmetoder](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=2018806) eller når du foretar et kjøp.

- [Legge til betalingsmetode](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/add-update-or-remove-credit-card-or-bank-account?view=o365-worldwide#add-a-payment-method)

- [Oppdatere en eksisterende betalingsmetode](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/add-update-or-remove-credit-card-or-bank-account?view=o365-worldwide#update-an-existing-payment-method)

- [Endre en betalingsmetode](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/add-update-or-remove-credit-card-or-bank-account?view=o365-worldwide#change-a-payment-method)

- [Fjerne en betalingsmetode](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/add-update-or-remove-credit-card-or-bank-account?view=o365-worldwide#remove-a-payment-method)

- [Feilsøke betalingsmetoder](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/add-update-or-remove-credit-card-or-bank-account?view=o365-worldwide#troubleshoot-payment-methods)
