2. [Sikre Mac programvare nedlastinger er aktivert i Office 365 Administrasjonsportal](https://support.office.com/article/c13051e6-f75c-4737-bc0d-7685dcedf360).
    
3. [Tilordne en lisens for Office for Mac-brukere](https://support.office.com/article/997596B5-4173-4627-B915-36ABAC6786DC).
    
4. Brukere kan deretter installere Office-programvaren selv, eller du kan bruke [Distribuere Office for Mac-brukere](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/DeployOffice/mac/deployment-guide-for-office-for-mac).
