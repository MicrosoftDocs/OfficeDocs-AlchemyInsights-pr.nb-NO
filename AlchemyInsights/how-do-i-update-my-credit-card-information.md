# <a name="how-do-i-update-my-credit-card-information"></a><span data-ttu-id="43151-102">Hvordan oppdaterer jeg kredittkortinformasjonen?</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="43151-102">How do I update my credit card information?</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="43151-103">Gå til **Fakturering** \> [Betalingsmetoder](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=842054)-siden i administrasjonssenteret for Microsoft 365.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="43151-103">In the Microsoft 365 admin center, go to the **Billing** \> [Payment methods](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=842054) page.</span></span>
